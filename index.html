Stand Up To Terrorism

Should ordinary civilians participate in the fight against terrorism?

Our Cause

Our organization focuses on the issue of terrorism, a growing threat in today's society, and provides relevant, useful information about it to all.

Purpose and Mission Statement

Our purpose is to raise awareness about this growing problem so that Americans can have better insights on global and domestic affairs, which allows them to make better decisions in life, thereby improving the society around them. We aim to achieve this goal by posting news reports and information, alongside petitioning political leaders and officeholders to make this a bigger issue in the United States.
